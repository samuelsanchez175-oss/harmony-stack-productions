Frequently asked questions

Tap a question to expand the answer.

QWhat is Penwork Studio?
Penwork Studio is a songwriting and lyric studio for writing, organizing, and refining songs — with BPM, key, scale, and syllable counts attached to each one.
QHow do I create a new song?
Tap the compose / + button on your catalog screen to start a new song, then give it a title and start writing.
QWhat do BPM, Key, Scale, and the syllable counter do?
They let you tag a song with its tempo, musical key, and scale, and the syllable counter shows the length of your lines so you can keep a consistent flow.
QCan I organize and search my songs?
Yes — use Folders, sort by BPM or creation date, and search across your whole catalog from the home screen.
QWhere are my songs stored?
Your songs are saved on your device. We recommend keeping your device backed up so your catalog is protected.
