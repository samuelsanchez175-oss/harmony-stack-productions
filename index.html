    Read before submitting changes.

    01
    Take Screenshots
    Go through the full preview carefully. Screenshot every area you want changed. Use Snipping Tool, Command+Shift+4 on Mac, or your phone.
    02
    Mark Them Up
    Circle, arrow, or annotate the exact element on each screenshot. Use Canva, Google Slides, iPhone Markup, or any annotation tool. Vague screenshots cause delays.
    03
    Write Full Context
    For each screenshot, write a clear explanation of what's wrong and what you want instead. "Make it bigger" is not enough. Reference a site or describe exactly what you mean.
    04
    Send One Complete List
    Compile everything into one message. Do not send revisions one at a time over multiple days. Review the full site, collect all changes, then send them together.
    One pass per revision round. Sending changes in pieces uses up rounds fast. Collect every change you want, review the entire site, then submit everything together in a single list. Rounds cannot be split or carried over — use them wisely.
