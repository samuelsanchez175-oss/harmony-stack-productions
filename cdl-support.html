Frequently asked

Answers to common questions

Which license and tests does the app cover?
CDL One Stop NJ focuses on the New Jersey CDL knowledge exam — Core Knowledge, Endorsements, Combination, and the Pre-Trip Inspection — across 13 study areas.
How does the readiness score work?
Your readiness percentage blends your accuracy with how much of each question bank you've actually seen. Reaching 100% means you've consistently passed practice across every area.
Is my progress saved?
Yes. Your streaks, levels, key dates, and missed questions are kept on your device so you can pick up where you left off.
How do I set my test date?
On the Study tab, tap "Set up your key dates" to add your exam date and study toward a countdown.
